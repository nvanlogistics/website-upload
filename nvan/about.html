About NVAN Logistics

NVAN Logistics Pvt Ltd is well positioned to provide prompt, efficient and secure transportation services as per your requirements. Since our early days, we have striven to be at the heart of the Indian business community by operating in the logistics industry. Our enterprising spirit is at the core of our identity and it manifests itself in various forms be it via pioneering new routes for long distance transport in the 2020’s or via employing the best fleet and personnel to deliver your cargo securely with today’s technology.
We started our transport business in 2004 with own fleet and handling on individual basis by Mr. Narendra Kumar Mishra, Mr. Veerendra Kumar Mishra and Mr. Nitesh Mishra. After successfully completion of 5 years, in year 2009 we started our sister concern Firms by the name of Shreenath Cargo Movers as a Transport Commission Agent. Following this, we kept on climbing the ladder of success and slowly we started our other sister Firms.
We, through our sister concerns, are providing efficient service to the nation through different sources since 2009, details of which are as follows:

  • Shreenath Cargo Movers, Gandhidham
  • Shreenath Cargo Movers, Delhi
  • Shreenath Carrier, Kanpur
  • Keshav Transport, Bokaro
  • Swift India Logistics
